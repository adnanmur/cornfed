Sustainable Future

Corn farmers are creating a sustainable world for future generations.

Growing a world for future generations.

As stewards of the land, corn farmers understand the responsibility to create a more environmentally and economically sustainable world for future generations through transparency and continued advances and efficiencies in land, water and energy use.

To continue their commitment to sustainability, corn farmers are constantly looking to the future and are committed to achieving the following by 2030:

Increase land use efficiency by 12%

Reducing soil erosion by 13%

Increasing irrigation water use efficiency by 15%

Increasing energy use efficiency by 13%

Reducing GHG emissions by 13%
